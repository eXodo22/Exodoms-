About Exodoms

Premium Cardamom Exports from Kerala

Located in the heart of Idukki's spice region in Vandenmedu, Kerala, Exodoms Spices Exports specializes in sourcing and exporting premium quality cardamom to international markets.

Though we are a year-old company, our commitment to quality and authentic sourcing has quickly established us as a reliable partner for wholesalers and retailers seeking genuine Indian cardamom.

We source directly from local plantations in the Idukki hills, ensuring freshness, authenticity, and the distinctive aroma that Kerala cardamom is famous for worldwide.
